# The American System of Creating Private Wealth

A book on how the United States economy actually works — reframing it as a
machine with a "control panel," challenging the scarcity / "deficit myth"
narrative, and arguing that national prosperity is constrained by *real
resources* (skilled workers, functioning factories, reliable supply chains),
not by money.

Written for the builders, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders who want a
diagnostic understanding of the economic system rather than the headlines —
the evidence and language to engage confidently with leaders and peers.
